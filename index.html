Features

Everything you need for serious research.

🔍

Smart Paper Search

Search millions of academic papers via CrossRef API with instant, relevant results filtered by topic, date, and citation count.

🤖

AI Summaries

Powered by Groq's ultra-fast LLM, ScholAris summarizes complex papers into clear, digestible insights in seconds.

☁️

Cloud Sync

Firebase Auth & Firestore keep your saved papers, notes, and collections perfectly synced across all your devices.

📚

Personal Library

Organize papers into custom collections, add your own notes, and build a structured research library that grows with you.

📱

Flutter Native

Built with Flutter for a smooth, native experience on both Android and iOS. Fast, responsive, and beautiful on any screen.

🔒

Secure & Private

Your data is yours. Firebase Authentication ensures only you can access your research. No ads, no tracking, no selling your data.
